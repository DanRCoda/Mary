Hi! I'm Mary Ryan.

I aspire to make the world a better place by helping people thrive in their careers. I have successfully coached clients at various stages in their careers ranging from high school seniors to C-Suite executives. I help people maximize their strenghts in their current position, and thoughtfully crush the job search when it's time for a change.

Most recently, I spent 4 years coaching working-professional MBA students and presenting virtual and in-person leadershp workshops to aduiences ranging from 5 to 200 participants.

Now I manage the US Early Career Program for a global electrification and robotics company. Marigold Career & Leadership Consulting is my labor of love. My work makes me feel alive, and I want the same for you!
